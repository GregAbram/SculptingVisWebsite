Sculpting Visualizations

Sculpting Visualizations is a multidisciplinary, cross-institutional team creating tools that bring artists and designers directly into the scientific visualization process. By integrating the arts and the sciences, the Sculpting Vis Collaborative aims to improve domain science research, team collaboration, and public-facing communication about important issues in climate, computing, neurology, archeology, biology, and more.
